Bulk Trackback & Comments On/Off for your posts or pages in a super easy way.

== Description ==
This plugin allows you to bulk edit trackbacks and comments on/off for your posts or pages with a single click on checkbox. In addition, you can also sort by those statuses.

== Installation ==
Upload plugin to your plugins folder (unzip) and activate it in WP admin.
